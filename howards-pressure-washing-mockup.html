FAQs

Questions we get before every job.

Is pressure washing safe for my roof, siding, and paint?+

Yes — we soft-wash delicate surfaces like roofing, siding, and painted wood using low pressure and the right cleaning solution, and reserve high pressure for concrete, brick, and other surfaces built to take it.

How much does a typical job cost?+

Pricing depends on square footage and surface type. We give a firm, written quote before any work starts — no surprise add-ons.

Are you insured?+

Yes, Howard's Pressure Washing is fully insured. Proof of coverage is available on request for both residential and commercial customers.

Do you offer sealing after the wash?+

Yes — we offer AggreSeal and SurfKoat sealing on exposed aggregate and concrete so the clean holds up against weather, staining, and UV wear for years, not months.

How soon can you get us on the schedule?+

Most residential jobs book within the week. Commercial clients with overnight or off-hours needs — restaurants, retail lots — get priority scheduling to avoid disrupting business hours.
